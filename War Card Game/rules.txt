2 Player game.
Each player gets half of the deck.
Ace Beats every card except for 2.
Players place down one card at a time, the player with the higher value card takes both cards.
When two cards of the same value are placed, both players place 3 cards face down 
and one card face up, whoever has the highest faceup card wins.
The first player to run out of cards loses.
